How do I pay?

Use Option 1 (scan QR) or Option 2 (copy UPI ID). Open your preferred UPI app, enter the plan amount manually, and complete payment.

What happens after payment?

Tap "Activate on Telegram" and send your payment screenshot. Your access will be activated after quick verification — usually instant.
